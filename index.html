About me

I am a creative, goal-oriented, enthusiastic young professional with plans to attend medical school in the near future. After graduating from the University of Florida in 2017 with a B.S. in Behavioral and Cognitive Neuroscience with cum laude honors, I relocated to the Denver, CO area. I have a wide range of experiences, including clinical, research, leadership, creative, and volunteer - available for viewing on my LinkedIn profile below. I hope to be be involved with technology in medicine, including documentation methods and medical decision making, as my career progresses

My interests are in problem-solving, programming, machine learning, health & medicine, graphic design, and entrepreneurship. My hobbies include enjoying my two dogs, Neptune and Griffin, American football (Florida Gators especially!), and the popular video game Rocket League.
